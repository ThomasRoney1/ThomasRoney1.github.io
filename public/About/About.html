Why start this?

The Why

High school can be challenging. The stress of trying to keep up with coursework, preparing for standardized tests like the SAT, and planning for college makes it easy to feel overwhelmed. I understand that these stresses can sometimes become overpowering, and I'm here to help lighten the load.

That's why I created this tutoring initiative. It's not about making money or building a business - it's about supporting students and ensuring that they can get the academic support they need. No expensive rates, no long-term contracts, just genuine peer-to-peer teaching and learning.

I started this initiative in honor of my late grandfather, a high school teacher who instilled in me the importance of learning. I hope to pass on what I have learned from him, and I hope that one day you will do the same.

I was taught that education isn't just about grades or SAT scores - it's about developing an understanding and fascination about the world that you will continue to nurture for the rest of your life. My Grandfather instilled that fascination within me, and every session I do, I hope to pass it on to you.
