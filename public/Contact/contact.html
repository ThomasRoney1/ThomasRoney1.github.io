Quick FAQ

Is this really free? +

Yep, completely free! This site was created so I could give back to the community. I'm doing this in honor of my grandfather and to help fellow students succeed.

What subjects do you help with? +

I focus on subjects I've personally taken and done well in. See the About Page for more specific details. If you need help with something else, just ask and I'll let you know if I can help!

How does the tutoring work? +

We can meet virtually whenever works for your schedule. Unfortunately, I am a student myself so will likely need weekends and some time on the weekdays to complete my own homework. My calendar shows my availability.

How quickly will you respond? +

Usually within a few hours during the day, sometimes faster! I check my messages pretty regularly, especially during school weeks. However, if you message while I am in class, you will likely need to wait until the end of the school day.

What if we don't click or it's not working? +

No worries at all! Not every tutoring match works perfectly, and that's totally normal. We can always adjust our approach or I can help you find someone else who might be a better fit.

A Few Quick Things to Know

This is completely free, no strings attached. I'm doing this because I genuinely want to help other students succeed. My goal is just to make your high school experience a little less stressful and maybe even fun sometimes!
